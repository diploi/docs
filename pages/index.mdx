# Introduction

### What is Diploi?

Diploi is a platform and workflow to make you and your development team both happy and efficient.  
We have turned every stone to make your life as a developer as simple as possible.

### Key Features

- Launch staging, development and production deployments with only a few clicks

- Runs stateful as well as stateless projects

- Develop without installing or keeping anything locally
